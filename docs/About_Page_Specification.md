## Page Overview

The About page (`/about`) serves as the heart of the Olive and Salt brand story, introducing visitors to the chef's personal journey and the deeper meaning behind the service. This page should convey warmth, authenticity, and professional expertise while maintaining the brand's elegant, grounded aesthetic.

---

## Content Structure

### 1. Page Header

- **Page Title**: "About Olive and Salt"
- **Subtitle**: "Where freshness meets personalization in your own kitchen"
- **Hero Image**: Warm, natural-lit photo of the chef in a kitchen setting or with fresh ingredients

### 2. Meet Your Chef Section

- **Section Title**: "Meet Your Chef"
- **Chef Photo**: Professional portrait with natural lighting, showcasing warmth and approachability
- **Bio Content**:
  - Personal background and culinary journey
  - Professional training and experience
  - Passion for fresh, seasonal ingredients
  - Philosophy on nourishing both body and soul
- **Cooking Philosophy Subsection**:
  - Emphasis on holistic nourishment
  - Commitment to personalization
  - Approach to in-home cooking experience

### 3. Why Olive and Salt Section

- **Section Title**: "Why Olive and Salt?"
- **Name Origin Story**:
  - Symbolism of olive (peace, nourishment, Mediterranean heritage)
  - Symbolism of salt (preservation, enhancement, essential element)
  - How these elements represent the service philosophy
- **Service Differentiators**:
  - In-home cooking advantage
  - Personalized meal planning
  - Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
  - Wellness-focused approach

### 4. Values Highlight

- **Visual representation of core values**:
  - **Freshness**: Seasonal, organic, locally sourced
  - **Personalization**: Tailored to individual needs
  - **Holistic Nourishment**: Wellness-focused cooking

---

## Layout Specifications

### Desktop Layout (1200px+)

- **Container**: Maximum width 1200px, centered
- **Section Spacing**: 80px vertical margins between sections
- **Grid System**:
  - Meet Your Chef: 40% image, 60% text (side-by-side)
  - Why Olive and Salt: Full-width text with centered alignment
  - Values: Three-column layout with icons

### Tablet Layout (768px - 1199px)

- **Container**: Full width with 40px side margins
- **Section Spacing**: 60px vertical margins
- **Meet Your Chef**: Stacked layout (image above text)
- **Values**: Two-column layout (third item spans full width)

### Mobile Layout (< 768px)

- **Container**: Full width with 20px side margins
- **Section Spacing**: 40px vertical margins
- **All sections**: Single column, stacked layout
- **Values**: Single column layout

---

## Typography Specifications

### Headings

- **Page Title**: Playfair Display, 48px (desktop), 36px (tablet), 32px (mobile)
- **Section Titles**: Playfair Display, 36px (desktop), 28px (tablet), 24px (mobile)
- **Subsection Titles**: Playfair Display, 24px (desktop), 20px (tablet), 18px (mobile)
- **Color**: Olive Green (#556B2F)
- **Line Height**: 1.2

### Body Text

- **Font**: Lato (primary choice) or Nunito (secondary)
- **Size**: 18px (desktop), 16px (tablet/mobile)
- **Color**: Olive Green (#556B2F) for main text
- **Line Height**: 1.6
- **Paragraph Spacing**: 20px bottom margin

### Accent Text

- **Font**: Lato/Nunito Italic
- **Size**: 16px
- **Color**: Sea Salt Gray (#A8A29E)
- **Use**: Photo captions, quotes, emphasis

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Performance

- **Image Optimization**: WebP format with fallbacks
- **Lazy Loading**: Images below the fold
- **Font Loading**: Font-display: swap for web fonts

### Responsive Behavior

- **Breakpoints**: 768px (tablet), 1200px (desktop)
- **Images**: Responsive with appropriate sizes for each breakpoint
- **Typography**: Fluid scaling between breakpoints

### Animation Considerations

- **Subtle Animations**: Fade-in on scroll for sections
- **Duration**: 300-500ms for transitions
- **Easing**: Ease-out for natural feel
- **Accessibility**: Respect prefers-reduced-motion
