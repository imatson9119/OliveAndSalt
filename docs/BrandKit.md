# Olive and Salt Brand Kit

## Overview

**Brand Name**: Olive and Salt  
**Founder**: Emma Matson  
**Tagline (optional)**: Food with Heart, Served at Home

Olive and Salt is a personal chef service dedicated to crafting memorable, customized meals using high-quality ingredients—all prepared in your home. The brand blends natural warmth with artistic detail, culinary storytelling, and a deep love of hospitality.

## Color Palette

| Purpose            | Color Name  | Hex       | Description                            |
| ------------------ | ----------- | --------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **Primary**        | Olive Green | `#637B2F` | Freshness, grounded nature, and health |
| **Secondary**      | Cream White | `#FDF9F3` | Purity, simplicity, negative space     |
| **Accent**         | Terracotta  | `#ED7B58` | Warmth, emphasis, creative flair       |
| **Light Neutral**  | Soft Gray   | `#F7F7F7` | Section backgrounds, airy layout       |
| **Medium Neutral** | Mid Gray    | `#969696` | Subtext, muted UI elements             |
| **Dark Neutral**   | Charcoal    | `#333333` | Primary text, strong contrast on cream |

> **Usage Tips**

- **Olive Green**: logo, buttons, links, titles
- **Cream White**: backgrounds, whitespace-heavy layouts
- **Terracotta**: hover effects, highlights, CTAs
- **Grays**: readable, flexible UI layers

---

## Typography

**Headlines**

- Font: `Playfair Display`, fallback: `Georgia, serif`
- Style: Elegant, slightly decorative serif
- Tone: Inviting, artistic, classic with a modern edge

**Body Text**

- Font: `Open Sans`, fallback: `Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif`
- Style: Clean, neutral sans-serif
- Tone: Friendly, clear, readable on all screens

> **Pairing Guidance**  
> Use serif headers to evoke warmth and craft. Sans-serif body keeps everything grounded and digestible. Maintain ample line spacing and paragraph breaks to reflect a relaxed, curated aesthetic.

## Iconography & UI Design

- Line-based, thin icons in charcoal or olive
- Rounded corners and soft drop shadows
- Buttons: Olive background with cream text, terracotta hover
- Forms: Minimal fields with clear labels, bordered inputs on cream backgrounds
