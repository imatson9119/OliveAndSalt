My Culinary Journey

With over 15 years of experience in professional kitchens and a Master's in Nutritional Science, I've dedicated my career to proving that healthy food doesn't have to sacrifice flavor. My journey began in France, where I trained in classical techniques, but it was my grandmother's garden in Northern California that taught me the true magic of fresh, seasonal ingredients.

After working in Michelin-starred restaurants in San Francisco and New York, I realized my passion wasn't just for creating exceptional food‚Äîit was for sharing that experience with people in their own homes. There's something deeply personal and nourishing about cooking in someone's kitchen, understanding their preferences, and creating meals that become part of their daily joy.

I founded Olive & Salt in 2019 with a simple mission: to bring restaurant-quality, nutrition-focused cuisine directly to your table, prepared in the comfort and privacy of your own kitchen.
